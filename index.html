Biography

Hey! I am Peng Zhang, currently a PhD student in Graduate School of Education at Peking University (PKU), and fortunate to be advised by Prof. Junjie Shang. Previously, I obtained my bachelor's degree from Beijing Normal University (BNU),fortunately advised by Prof. Su Cai.


My research interests fall in Learning Sciences, sicence education, and virtual learning environment. The current research focuses on gamified learning environment.
